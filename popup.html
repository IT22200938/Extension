Privacy & Consent

This extension tracks user interactions on web pages. Your data is stored locally and never sent to external servers without your explicit action.

  • Mouse clicks and movements
  • Keyboard interactions (keys masked for privacy)
  • Form submissions (no form data captured)
  • Page views and scroll behavior
